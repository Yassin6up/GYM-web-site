Why GYM

Does sports affect my mind ?

At the same time, physical activity stimulates production of endorphins. These are natural mood lifters that can help keep stress and depression at bay. Endorphins may also leave you feeling more relaxed and optimistic after a hard workout on the field

W is the role of the gym in building the body ?

One purpose of a gym is to help you learn new exercises, techniques and training methods to meet your weight-loss, muscle building, heart health or sports performance goals

Why is sport important ?

Clearly, sports can help you reach your fitness goals and maintain a healthy weight. However, they also encourage healthy decision-making such as not smoking and not drinking. Sports also have hidden health benefits such as lowering the chance of osteoporosis or breast cancer later in life.

Why do you do weight lifting exercises even though you want to lose weight ?

There is incorrect information circulating in sports circles stating that exercises during which weightlifting is done do not burn fat and are not useful, but rather make fat accumulate on the muscles and distort the appearance of the body. Of course, this is not true at all. There is nothing to burn fat, increase the speed of burning inside the body, and ensure its continuity, better than weight lifting exercises by combining them with fitness exercises and not only jogging and cycling in the gym.
